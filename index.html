Its nice to meet you.

I am a UX Designer and Responsive Web Developer with more than 3 years of industry work experience.

​

I build bridges between users and businesses with human centered innovations for apps and products. I am a people-person that thrives in collaborative environments. I absolutely love people watching. Observing everyday interactions often reveals subtle details about how we relate to the natural world.

I currently live in Dallas, Texas and work for Booz Allen Hamilton, specializing in front-end web development for their Strategic Innovation Group. My love for coding led me to UX design and further exploring the intersection of design and technology.

Web Design Process

UX Research

I ask lots of annoying questions. Typically, lots of "Why's?". Although I ask lots of questions, I also listen and watch. I use empathic design techniques throughout my process in order to reveal the values and aspirations of users.

design & Testing

You will probably never get the best design at first try. After all, design is iterative. In a first design meeting I typically get users engaged in a design thinking exercise.

Development

Design now days is often about being a hybrid thinker. We need to be able to connect the dots between what’s culturally desirable, technically feasible, and viable from a business point of view. I use my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks to manipulate website designs and web content.
