Personal Website Logo Design

Visual/Graphic Design

Project Overview

Challenge:

Create brand identity for my personal website.

Solution:

Create a custom logo for my personal website.

Role:

Designer, Researcher

Tool:

Pen, Paper, Illustrator

Background

I decided to challenge myself and create a logo for my personal website. I wanted it to be a combination of my interests: turtles, technology, soccer, the beach, nature, mud runs, and a multitude of other things. It was a difficult design process for me considering I have a lot of interests and I also wanted the logo to be simple. I decided that I loved turtles the most. I then proceeded to search hundreds of famous logos on Google, until my brain hurt from my many, many, Google Chrome tabs.

Try, Try, Again

I started by sketching and brainstorming my ideas on paper. I knew that I wanted a turtle to be the centerpiece of the logo. I also considered incorporating my name or initials in a creative way.

My sketches looked more like drawings than a logo, so I started creating designs in Illustrator for more graphic outcomes. Below are just a few of the iterations during my logo creation process.

The Result

My final logo design exhibits my love for turtles and technology. The circuit tracing idea came from a friend once I showed them my sketches and Illustrator art. I then spent hours on Google images looking for circuit drawings that I can use to practice with. The idea of having my name as a closing tag in HTML came once I went to a tech event in DC. Once, I finalized my logo, I added it to my personal site navigation and favicon.

Through this process, I learned that logo design takes time and inspiration from my world around me. Although my logo isn't perfect, its something Im proud to say I created myself.
