Project Overview

Challenge:

Client needs a responsive personal website with great user experience in time for music video.

Solution:

Design and implement responsive site that meets client's need with a short turn around.

Role:

Content strategy, Information Architecture, Web Development

Tool:

Atom, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Github.io

Client Requirements

My client is a friend that wanted a music site built for himself. He is a rapper that constantly had new music, pictures, and events. He wanted to have one specific place to put all his content and still have the option to upload to social media platforms. He wanted, "an online headquarters for his music".

He wanted the site to be linked directly to his music (Youtube videos and Soundcloud) as well as to pictures and annouced shows. He doesn't want to worry about whether or not people are actually seeing his updates, or if the information is getting lost in the shuffle. Anything he post will be right there on the site until he doesn't want it to be anymore.

Logo & Design

My client expressed to me that he would prefer his logo be simple and straight to the point. He wanted something that easily communicated his brand. He also wanted a theme with the colors: black, red, blue, and white emphasized throughout the site. There was a large amount of content specifically in the Bio section. I used various content strategy techniques to reduce the amount of content and retain the important information.

My client requested a list of ten quotes from his music that he wanted displayed on the site. I decided to have the quotes appearing one by one in the center of the site, in order to once again avoid displaying too much text for the user at once, causing a cognitive overload.

The Results & What's Next

After a few rounds of communicating changes back and forth, my client had his site ready two weeks prior to his video shoot. I also helped make the site live by using Github.io as a hosting enviroment for the site. He already had a desired domain name, so I just went online to ensure that the domain was available and secured the domain name via GoDaddy. I also created a github repository so that the code can easily be managed and updated efficently.

In the future, I will have "View More" buttons in the "Videos" and "Shows" sections that will navigate to another page within the site that will show more youtube videos or shows content. Once I am provided with more videos and information I will add these features.
